Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Numble ("the App") values your privacy. This policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or phone number. However, to serve ads, our advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect the following:

  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA / Advertising ID)
  • Device information and approximate location (country level)
  • App usage and ad interaction data

2. How We Use Information

  • To display personalized or non-personalized ads
  • To improve app stability and analyze errors

3. Advertising & Third-Party Services

The App serves ads through Google AdMob. For details on how AdMob processes data, see the Google Privacy Policy. On iOS, in accordance with App Tracking Transparency (ATT), advertising identifiers are used for tracking only with your consent.

4. Data Retention & Storage

User settings such as game progress are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to any external server.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not primarily directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You can reset your advertising identifier or limit personalized ads in your device settings. On iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. On Android: Settings > Google > Ads.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at numworks.contact@gmail.com.
